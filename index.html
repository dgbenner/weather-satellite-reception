Meteor-M Highland Park Station

Live weather satellite imagery captured from Highland Park, St. Paul, Minnesota. This station receives transmissions from Russian polar-orbiting weather satellites Meteor-M N°2-3 (137.1 MHz) and Meteor-M N°2-4 (137.9 MHz) as they pass overhead, broadcasting Low Rate Picture Transmission (LRPT) data.

Typical pass frequency: 2-3 visible passes per day per satellite
